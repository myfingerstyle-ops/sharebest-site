## Build around your main mess

Pet owners usually deal with three common problems: loose hair, muddy paws, and odor control. Start with the problem that happens most often in your home.

## Useful tool categories

### For pet hair

Lint removers, rubber brushes, and lightweight handheld vacuums work well for quick surface cleanup.

### For floors and entry areas

Absorbent mats, paw wipes, and compact spray cleaners help reduce repeat messes.

### For soft surfaces

Fabric-safe odor sprays and washable covers can make sofas, beds, and crates easier to maintain.

## Bottom line

The best setup is usually a small group of easy-to-use tools placed where messes happen most often.
